# Public-health and safety boundary

This project is for research and environmental safety review support only.

It must not be used as:

- an official boil-water advisory system,
- a drinking-water safety certification tool,
- a treatment-plant operating directive,
- a public-health emergency notification system,
- a replacement for laboratory confirmation,
- a substitute for utility, regulator, or public-health authority decisions.

Real deployment requires certified sensors, calibration, laboratory confirmation, utility standard operating procedures, regulator review, accessibility review, public communication governance, incident response planning, and human expert oversight.
